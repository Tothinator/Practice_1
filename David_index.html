Making a PB&J

  1. Gather your ingredients
    • Peanut butter
    • Jelly
    • Two pieces of bread
  2. Use the knife to spread jelly on one side of a piece of bread
  3. Wipe the remaining jelly on the 2nd piece of bread
  4. Use the knife to spread peanut butter on the 2nd piece of bread
  5. Combine and enjoy!
